Venue

ISBRA2021 will take place at Genpla Shenzhen Hotel(深圳君璞酒店), located at NO.3333 Liuxian Blvd, Nanshan District, Shenzhen 518052, China.
Check the official hotel website for more details: http://www.genplahotel.com/

Transportation

Metro and taxi are available from Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport/ Shenzhen North Railway Station to and from Genpla Shenzhen Hotel.
1. Transportation options from Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport
Taxi: Show to the driver “请带我去西丽塘朗城君璞酒店”. The trip takes ~50minutes(30km)
Metro: Airport Station->Line11->Qianhaiwan Station->Line5->Tanglang Station(A). The trip takes ~50 minutes.
2. Transportation options from Shenzhen North Railway Station:
Taxi： Show to the driver “请带我去西丽塘朗城君璞酒店”. The trip takes ~12minutes(5km)
Metro： Shenzhen North Railway Station->Line5->Tanglang Station(A). The trip takes ~12 minutes.
